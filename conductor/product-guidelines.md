# Product Guidelines: 时光书函 (Time Letter)

## Narrative Tone & Voice
- **The "Poetic Humility" Blend:** The writing style must seamlessly transition between **Poetic Romance** (used for high-level reflections and grand milestones) and **Casual Humor** (used for raw, authentic, and "imperfect" moments).
- **Harmonized Perspective:** While presenting two sides of the story, the voices should feel **harmonized**. The goal is not to contrast personalities, but to emphasize the shared frequency and deep connection between "家灏 & 佩佩."

## Visual Aesthetic: Modern Chinese Style (现代中国风)
- **Philosophy:** Balance, restraint, and subtle elegance.
- **Color Palette:** Focus on traditional-inspired tones (e.g., cinnabar red for accents, ink-wash greys, or warm paper whites) but applied within a clean, modern digital interface.
- **Typography:** Use high-quality Serif fonts for storytelling (evoking the feel of a letter) and clean San-Serif for functional elements. Chinese characters should be treated with the respect of calligraphy—balanced and impactful.
- **Layout:** Embrace **Minimalism**. Use generous white space ("Meaningful Gaps") to allow the photos and words to breathe.

## Storytelling Mechanics
- **Narrative Reflection:** "Mirror Moments" should be highlighted through the writing. When a current event echoes a past one, the text should explicitly reflect on that growth or change, creating a recursive layer to the timeline.
- **Raw Authenticity:** Do not shy away from "imperfect" photos. The guidelines prioritize shots that capture real laughter, genuine grit, and the "messy" beauty of travel and life.

## Emotional Target
- **Warmth & Joy:** Every design and content decision should contribute to a feeling of pervasive warmth. The reader should feel the "warmth of shared growth" and the steady joy of a journey taken together.
