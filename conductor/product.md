# Initial Concept
时光书函 (Time Letter) is a personalized vertical timeline story web gallery built for "家灏 & 佩佩" to document their journey together. It aims to transcend a simple photo album by creating a deeply moving, interactive digital narrative.

# Product Guide: 时光书函 (Time Letter)

## Vision
To create a SOTA (State-of-the-Art) interactive digital narrative that captures the essence of a shared journey. This project is not just a gallery; it is a "digital情书" (digital love letter) designed to transport the reader into the emotional world of its protagonists through a sophisticated dual-perspective narrative.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Personal use for "家灏 & 佩佩" to relive their memories.
- **Secondary:** Close friends and family invited to glimpse their journey.

## Core Narrative Strategy (SOTA Approach)
- **Narrative Transportation Theory:** Leveraging immersive visuals and psychological pacing to pull the reader deep into the story.
- **Dual-Perspective POV:** Simultaneously presenting the internal monologues and feelings of both protagonists. This creates a "dialogue across time and space" that reveals the hidden depths of shared moments.
- **Emotional Arc:** Moving beyond simple chronological logging to structure the timeline around significant emotional milestones—from the "Inciting Incident" (the beginning) to "Deepening Connection" and "Mirror Moments."

## Key Design Principles
- **Immersive Viewing:** A high-quality, full-screen interactive experience where photos and stories take center stage.
- **Meaningful Gaps:** Using intentional white space and minimal text sections to allow the weight of visual emotions to settle with the reader.
- **Mirror Moments:** Actively identifying and visually linking emotional or visual parallels across the timeline (e.g., comparing the first meeting to a recent revisit of the same place).
- **Raw Authenticity:** Prioritizing "imperfect" but deeply real moments—candid shots, "spicy hotpot" laughter, and the grit of travel—over overly curated or "perfect" aesthetics.

## Success Criteria
The project is successful if a reader finishing the gallery feels a profound sense of the "weight of time" and the "warmth of shared growth."
