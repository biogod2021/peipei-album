# Track Specification: Curation & Integration via Media-Explorer

## Overview
This track aims to fulfill the "Narrative Completion Plan" by systematically selecting and integrating approximately 20-25 supplementary images from the `media-explorer` tool into the `peipei-album` project.

## Objectives
- Fill temporal gaps in the timeline (May, Aug, Sept 2025).
- Add "B-Roll" narrative texture (tickets, environmental details).
- Incorporate "Her POV" shots to create a dual-perspective narrative.
- Add "Physical Evidence" shots (collages of tickets/notes).
- Include "Mirror Moments" for temporal contrast.

## Target Content
1. **Daily Fragments (5-8 shots):** Cafeteria lunches, study desks, sunsets.
2. **Narrative "Breathing" (4-5 shots):** Travel tickets, environmental close-ups (e.g., flowers on Huangshan).
3. **Her POV (4-6 shots):** Candid shots of the user working, taking photos, or helping.
4. **Physical Evidence (2-3 shots):** Flat lays of movie tickets, handwritten notes.
5. **Mirror Moments (2 shots):** Comparative shots (First meeting vs. Latest revisit).

## Integration Strategy
- Use `media-explorer`'s tagging system to mark relevant photos.
- Extract photos and their metadata.
- Move photos to `public/assets/img/stories/` under a new `00-日常碎片` or specific event folders.
- Ensure all photos are converted to web-friendly formats (.jpg).
